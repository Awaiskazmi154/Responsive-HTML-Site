Things to See and Do

Ground Floor

From the main entrance to this area, there is level access. The route is 750mm
wide, or more.
▪ The door is 750mm wide..
▪ Some display information is low for wheelchair users and there are seats.
▪ The flooring is flagstones which are well maintained.
The member of staff will be wearing a uniform and name badge so they can be
easily identified. Staff are happy to provide assistance and talk to visitors about
the history of the House.
There is no background music or tannoy. The floor is carpeted and level
throughout.

First Floor

▪ From the main entrance to this area there are 11 steps. There is no lift and no
ramp..
▪ The wooden staircase to the first floor is 730mm wide with a 180 degree turn half
way up the steps. There is a low level ceiling 1710mm high. The steps are steep
with narrow treads. There is a handrail on the top flilght.
▪ At the top of the staircase there is a doorway to the small gallery. The door is
660mm wide and 1760mm high. The room has a peaked roof with beams at
1760mm high. The gallery displays and exhibition which changes annually.

Video Room

From the main entrance to this area, there is level access.
▪ The door is 780mm wide.
▪ The video room is attached to the main House but has its
own entrance from the
garden. A short film about the Revolution of 1688 lasts
approximately 10 minutes.
▪ Seating is provided but the room can only take a
maximum of 10 people. Seating
can be moved or rearranged to accommodate wheelchairs
and pushchairs.

Revolution House Garden

From the main entrance to the garden there is level access.
▪ The route is 820mm wide, or more.
▪ The route through the shop is 1030mm wide or more.
▪ There is a small, well maintained garden at the front of
the House with a grassed area, flowers and shrubs. Seating is provided and garden games are available on the lawn. There is a flagstone path from the gate along the front of the House. The garden is also used for special event days which can be busy. We provide a gazebo for shade or shelter at events.
